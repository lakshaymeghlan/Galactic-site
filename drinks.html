RASTEAU

Product Details

The Rasteau enjoys a variety of different soil types. Clay-limestone soil, bare sandy loam soils and red sandstone soils. As with the neighbouring land of Châteauneuf-du-Pape, many plots are covered in rounded quartz and limestone pebbles. These brown, limestone soils are well-suited to Syrah, Grenache, Mourvèdre, Carignan and Cinsault for the reds. The white wines are made from Clairette, Roussanne, Grenache Blanc and Bourboulenc. The Plan de Dieu terrace allows more powerful wines designed to be aged for longer to be grown.
